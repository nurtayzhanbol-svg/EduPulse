Platform Capabilities

Everything a teacher
needs to see

EduPulse works silently in the background, turning invisible student behaviour into clear, actionable insights — without adding complexity to your classroom.

Real-Time Monitoring
Every student's progress, idle time, and error rate streams live to your dashboard. Know instantly who is working and who is stuck.
AI Hint Engine
When a student stalls, the system automatically delivers a progressive hint — from a concept nudge to a structural clue — without giving the answer away.
Academic Integrity
Large paste events are detected the moment they happen. Your dashboard flags them immediately so you can follow up in the right way.
Confusion Detection
When several students stall at the same time, EduPulse spots the cluster and alerts you — so you can pause and re-explain before the class loses the thread.
Understanding Score
Each student receives a composite score updated every few seconds, weighing progress, accuracy, hint usage, and engagement into one clear number.
Post-Class Report
At the end of every session, EduPulse generates a structured summary — which topics were hardest, who excelled, and who needs a follow-up conversation.
